# 📊 Airline Customer Loyalty & Flight Activity Analysis

> 🚀 Analyzing travel patterns and loyalty tiers to maximize customer lifetime value (CLV)

---

## ⚡ (Quick Summary)

- 📈 **28% growth in flight activity (2017–2018)**
- 💎 **Aurora tier leads with $10,672 Avg. CLV**
- 📍 **75% of customers concentrated in top 3 cities**
- 📅 **Summer peaks identified (July)**
- 💡 Built actionable strategies to convert Star members to higher-value tiers

---

## 📌 Business Problem
The airline needs to understand the ROI of its loyalty program and identify which customer segments drive the most revenue through flight frequency and point accumulation.

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Evaluate performance across Star, Nova, and Aurora loyalty tiers
- Analyze seasonal flight trends to optimize marketing and operations
- Identify high-value geographic hubs for targeted promotions

---

## 🧠 Approach
✔ Data Cleaning & ETL (Excel)  
✔ Data Modeling (Power BI)  
✔ Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)  
✔ Customer Segmentation  
✔ Interactive Dashboard Development (Power BI)  

---

## 📊 Key Insights

- 🔴 **Star tier holds 46% of the customer base**
- 🟢 **Aurora members generate 58% higher CLV than Star members**
- 🔵 **Toronto is the largest hub (3,351 customers)**
- 🟡 **Average salary is consistent ($79k) across all tiers**
- 🟡 **Significant flight surge in July and December**
- 🟡 **Point redemptions increased by 18% YoY**

---

## ⚠️ Key Business Gaps

- ❌ Underutilized Star tier (High volume, low individual CLV)
- ❌ Seasonal capacity strain during summer peaks
- ❌ Lower engagement in smaller geographic regions
- ❌ Salary does not predict tier; behavior does

---

## 💡 Recommendations

- ✅ **Upsell Star members** to Nova through milestone bonuses
- ✅ **Targeted summer campaigns** for Toronto and Vancouver hubs
- ✅ **Enhance redemption options** during off-peak months
- ✅ **Focus on retention** for the high-value Aurora segment

---

## 📈 Business Impact (Expected)

📉 Reduced transition friction to premium tiers  
📈 Optimized seasonal flight load factor  
💰 Increased overall Customer Lifetime Value
